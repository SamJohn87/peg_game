Objective:

The objective of the Triangle Peg Game is to remove as many pegs as possible by jumping over them with other pegs, until no more moves are possible, or you've cleared the board except for one peg.

Select where to start game.

Please select any one of the holes on the board as your starting point for the peg game.

Just tap or click on the hole to begin.

Gameplay:

  • Click or tap on a peg to move it over an adjacent peg to a vacant hole .

  • The peg that was jumped over is removed from the board.

  • When there are multiple possible moves for the same peg, simply tap or click on the green hole to execute the desired move.
