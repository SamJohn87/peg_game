How to play Peg Solitaire Game?

Objective:

The objective of the English Peg Solitaire game is to remove as many pegs as possible by jumping over them with other pegs, until no more moves are possible, or you've cleared the board except for one peg.

Gameplay:

  • Pegs can only move by jumping over adjacent pegs to vacant holes.
  • You can jump horizontally or diagonally over an adjacent peg to an empty hole.
  • The peg that was jumped over is removed from the board.
